# Characters
## Dart
Our main character. This is a player character from the Neon City Overdrive campaign that I am running. He will be our PoV character in this game.

Dart is an Edgerunner (mercenary) operating in Sound City. He's in his mid to late 40s. He has no cybernetic enhancements at all. He's a bit of a smooth-talker. He has some past ties to gangs, though he's been trying to pay off his debts and escape "the life." 

Dart has a cousin named "Big Mike," who owns "Thunderbeers" which is a dive bar popular with Edgerunners in the area.

## Maya Wen
Dart's love interest. Maya is in her early 50s. She has a daughter, Audrey, who is in college.

She's a doctor of chemistry and works for a mega-corp. She met Dart when she was kidnapped from her commuter train by Dart and his crew. They needed her to help them gain access to some secure shipping containers that her company had sent out to one of their customers. Dart and his crew managed to convince her to help them, and in exchange they gave her a cut of their proceeds as well as getting her boss removed (which led to her being promoted).

## Jax Thorne
One of the gang members who Dart ran afowl of in the past. He's now working for Gallowglass, a mercenary company based out of Pacific Crown. Dart's crew seems to be the target of Gallowglass, or at least the team that Jax runs.

# Synopsis
The story of this game will be about Dart taking Maya on a date. After their date, Maya may choose to invite Dart over to introduce him to Audrey. The story will end with Maya, and possibly Audrey, being kidnapped by Jax Thorne in order to lure Dart and his crew into a Gallowglass trap (which will be played out in the TTRPG).
